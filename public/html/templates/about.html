Ezra Klitsie

(aka. voidkandy)

Spent 5 years as a full time musician before realizing that software and machine learning was better suited for his psychologic makeup.


What started as a love for guitar and music composition became a passion for production and sound design. Ableton was an excellent gateway drug into software. It was hard to tell if he was more interested in the music or in pushing the DAW as far as he could. After completing a multimedia music project and spending months grinding self promotion on social media, he was dissatisfied. Years of releases had not resulted in the music career he naively thought it would, something had to change. The release of ChatGPT led to the discovery of Python, because of the chatbot, programming became much more accessible. Pair programming stupid little .py scripts with GPT-3.5 eventually led to a fascination with machine learning. That fascination led to a genuine attempt to create his own models until the unfortunate reality of his lack of software knowledge got in the way. Since then he’s been obsessed with expanding his knowledge of software. Multiple personal projects and a newfound love of the Rust programming language leaves a job where he’s surrounded by much more talented developers as the only acceptable future on the horizon.
